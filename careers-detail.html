Engineering Manager, Native Apps

Outreach Lead

Kiev, UA

MP is home to a thriving community of games creators and backers who, together, are defining the future of how games are made. We’re always seeking new opportunities to elevate the most inventive projects coming to life on the platform, and we’re now hiring a digital strategist with a Games background to join us in our work.

About the Team

The Community Outreach team focuses on engaging with creators in the global creative communities we serve. We act as experts and advocates in our fields. Managing a large portfolio of relationships, we inspire creators to use MP/Drip to support their creative work. We are a team of entrepreneurial individuals who are action-oriented and believe deeply in the importance of creative independence.

About the Role

As a full-time Digital Outreach Lead on the Games team, you’ll have the responsibility of elevating the most exciting games projects on MP. You are able to write on-brand, ready-to-publish copy that elevates games creators’ projects and stories. You’ll help grow and optimize our existing newsletter and social channels — and you’ll develop new channels (like Twitch, Reddit, and Discord) to ensure that creators and backers are able to discover the most exciting and excellent games projects possible based on their interests. In your day to day, you’ll produce content for our social media, blog and newsletters, curate features, and develop streaming video content.

In This Role, You Will:

  • Be part of the Games team, which works directly with Games creators to help them succeed both on and off of the platform
  • Work closely with the Editorial team to elevate projects and surface creator stories that align with our vision, mission and brand
  • Drive backer engagement initiatives and strategies for the Games community; own Games channel-optimization initiatives and track engagement metrics to increase performance

Collaborate on and support Editorial team initiatives, which include Creative Prompts (like Make 100 and MP Gold), social media campaigns, homepage features, content partnerships, editorial features, and beyond

  • You are proficient in video streaming production
