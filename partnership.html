Consulting

While businesses have a wide range of skills and opportunities, they are not always enough to cover all the needs of the company, a certain project or employees. Therefore, they have to involve consultants who have experience in a necessary branch and have worked with other companies, so they can provide effective recommendations. A professional consultant will assist you in diagnosing problems and solving them, realize latent opportunities, which you might have never notice before, provide an objective review of a certain area of your business or project. We have analyzed the questions and request which we get most often and have established four possible directions in consulting partnership with Engre.

Financial consulting: you can hardly imagine any project that has no financial side. While people involved in it are, undoubtedly, good specialists in their field, they might face difficulties when trying to solve some financial issues. Here are some of the services, which you can get with us:

  • taxation;
  • dividends;
  • fiscal audit;
  • assessment;
  • financial analytics.

Legal consulting: We provide legal counsel regarding national and international legislation to the engineering sector. You can rest assured that our team of international lawyers with experience in applying international legislation will deliver customised advice relevant to your business situation.

Project management consulting: We provide management services for your projects across the full project life cycle. You can get your project estimated by our experts to be aware of necessary resources, costs and duration. Our level of service is extremely high including 24/7 support, regular and transparent reporting. We also provide support to manage the project efficiently including a visualisation of the deadline, agile project management and we are supported by tools including office 365 and sharepoint.

Business Projects

Do you really need a partner when you start running a business project in engineering services? Yes, you do if you:

  • - are looking for ideas where to find funds for the project;
  • - are not sure whether to buy or to rent engineering software;
  • - need to find experienced and motivated personnel that will match your project’s narrow specifications;
  • - have no experience or time to deal with technical documentation;
  • - would like to sell or rent your licensed industrial software to other companies.

All kinds of support including legal, financial, accounting and marketing consulting are provided for you as a partner of Engre. Do not let your initiatives sink in oblivion as all the issues like investments in your ideas, talent identification, providing you with contacts and database of partners are easily solved. Engre is a place to get things done.

Commercial

Partnership in advertising is important as never before. We can assist you to connect with your target audience like engineers or manufactures on a deeper level due to our experience in this field.

Once you are at the point when you need advertising, you do not have to think about annoying pop-ups that disrupt customer’s attention. Our marketing team will help you to build a different strategy of quality commercials: to state your business purpose, determine the needs of the market and make your services suitable and attractive for your customers.

One of the sides of the possible cooperation with Engre as a commercial partner is an access to our customers and partner’s database which will help you to find new sales channels, investors or associates to bring your ideas to life and make your goals achieved. Also, we provide an assistance in creation of marketing materials for your project and distributing them so you can stay in touch with your customers.

We are collecting feedbacks and suggestions from our customers, so let us know your opinion and what would you like to receive from this tool.
